klettrack

klettrack is a free, open-source app to log boulder and rock climbing sessions, plan training, and sync your boards (Tension Board, Kilter). Your data stays on your device - no accounts, no tracking.

Climbing Log

Record your climbs, grades, and attach videos - across gyms, boards, and styles. A clean log book to help you understand your climbing better.

Board Sync

Sync your Tension Board 2 and Kilter Board sessions into klettrack, giving you one place where all your climbs live.

Training Plans

Template-based sessions with a catalog of 100+ drills - climbing-specific strength, core, antagonist training, and mobility.

Analytics

See sends vs. attempts, send ratio, seasonality heatmaps, and more. Drill into your data to understand weaknesses and areas to focus on.

Timer

A proper training timer with get-ready, work, rest, and between-set intervals. Comes with presets for popular climbing and strength protocols, so you don’t have to juggle multiple apps.

Private by Design

All data stays on device. No accounts. No tracking. No ads.

Why klettrack?

We built the app we wanted: open, private, and focused on the essentials. No clutter. No gimmicks. Just a reliable way to track your climbing and training, whether you’re on a board, in the gym, or outside on rock.
